# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 25 May 2026_

This documentation site uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics and
advertising purposes, as described below. By continuing to use the site you
consent to the practices described here.

## Cookies and third-party advertising

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your
prior visits to this website and other websites. Google's use of advertising
cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to
this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting any of the following:

- [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- [Network Advertising Initiative](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/)
- [Your Online Choices (EU/UK)](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)

For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, see Google's
advertising policy at [policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

### GDPR and ePrivacy consent

If you are visiting from the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or
Switzerland, a consent banner (powered by Google Funding Choices) will ask
whether you allow personalized advertising and analytics cookies before any
such cookies are set. You can change your selection at any time using the
"Manage privacy choices" control surfaced by that banner.

## Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID `G-DSFH2NT388`) to understand how
visitors discover and use the site. GA4 stores a cookie containing a randomly
generated identifier and reports aggregate information about page views,
session duration, device class, country and referrer.

We have IP-anonymization enabled (`anonymize_ip: true`), so the last octet of
your IP address is dropped before any data is stored. We do not link analytics
data to any other identifier. See Google's data-processing terms at
[policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## What we do **not** collect

- We do not run any login, account or registration flow.
- We do not store form submissions, chat messages or any user-supplied text.
- The interactive playground runs entirely in your browser; nothing you type
  or toggle in it is sent to our servers.

## Data sharing

The only third parties that receive data from this site are:

- **Google** — Analytics (GA4) and advertising (AdSense, Funding Choices).
- **Netlify** — our hosting provider; receives standard HTTP request logs.

We do not sell or share your personal information for any purpose beyond what
is described above.

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

The "Last updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent
